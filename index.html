About the game:

The game is an expansion on the game Rock, Paper, Scissors. Its a game that has been created in the show : "Big Bang Theory"

Rules:

The rules are as follows:

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors
